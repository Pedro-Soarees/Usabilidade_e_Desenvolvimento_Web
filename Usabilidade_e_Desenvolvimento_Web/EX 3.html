Lista - Exercício 3

SPAGHETTI AGLIO E OLIO

MUST HAVES

* Spaghetti

* Extra virgin olive oil

* Garlic

* Salt

* Peperonccino

HOT TO MAKE

Bring a large pot of water to a boil.

Salt the water well (with about 1 tablespoon of kosher salt).

Peel and thinly slice 8 garlic cloves.

add the pasta and cook according to package instructions

Before you drain the pasta, reserve 1 1/2 cups of the pasta’s cooking water.

Warm 1/2 cup of extra virgin olive oil in a large pan over medium heat and Add the garlic

When the garlic has just started to brown, add 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes

Add the cooked pasta and stir until it’s nicely coated in the sauce and cooked to your liking.

Buon appetito!!
